Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

  1. What is collected. To run the game, the App stores gameplay data tied to your Reddit username / user ID: your sealed choice and stake each night, season points, streaks, participation history, and any Saint / Serpent flair standing.
  2. Where it is stored. All data is kept in Reddit-hosted storage (Redis) scoped to the installed subreddit. The App makes no external network requests — external HTTP is disabled in its configuration — so no game data is sent to the Developer or any third party.
  3. What is NOT collected. The App does not collect your email, real name, password, IP address, precise location, or any data beyond the Reddit-provided username/ID and the gameplay actions above. There is no analytics SDK, no tracking, and no advertising.
  4. No AI. The App performs no runtime AI processing of your data.
  5. Sharing. Data is not sold or shared. Your individual sealed choice is kept private until the nightly settlement and is only ever revealed as part of an aggregate split; comments the App files on your action are public within the subreddit, like any Reddit comment.
  6. Retention & deletion. Gameplay data persists while the App is installed. It is handled under Reddit’s platform data practices and the Reddit Privacy Policy. To request removal of content you created, contact a subreddit moderator or the Developer.
  7. Reddit’s policy. Your use of Reddit is governed by the Reddit Privacy Policy.
  8. Contact. Questions? Message u/edycutjong on Reddit.
